My Journey

I started with web development in 2018 and have not stopped since. I thought I was a big cheese when I learned to use the console to print "I pity the fool"

I am still coding though and over the over course of the last few years have managed to assemble a few projects. I am continuing to learn every day.

Services

Web development

I am trying to cater for small businesses that a need an online presence. Simple websites that work well is my main goal.

Online shops

I can work with businesses to create a local web shop that customers can buy items online. I work with stripe payment that does not charge an arm and a leg for commissions. See my shop project below to get an idea of what I could offer

Fixing websites

I am no expert but I can always take a look at your website or web project and see if I can help. Give me a call or message I am always glad to try and help.
